Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within the US typically takes 5-7 business days. We also offer expedited 2-day shipping options at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy. Products must be in original condition with all packaging intact.

How do I contact customer service?

For the fastest response, use the contact form below or email us directly at support@zentech.com.

Are ZenTech products cross-platform compatible?

Yes, all our input devices and power accessories are designed to be plug-and-play compatible with macOS, Windows, Linux, and most Android/iOS devices.

Do you offer corporate or bulk discounts?

Absolutely. Please contact us via the form below with the quantity and product types you are interested in, and our sales team will respond within 48 hours.

How do I pair the Zen Keyboard with multiple devices?

The Zen Keyboard supports up to three simultaneous Bluetooth connections. Use the dedicated FN + 1, 2, or 3 keys to switch instantly between paired devices.

What is the Zen AI Copilot trained on?

The Copilot uses an advanced language model trained on our entire product knowledge base, support tickets, and general market data to provide relevant shopping assistance and product comparison.
